the bizz is one of the world's most important business awards.it gathers and recognizes leading businesses from different regions that contribute to the daily growth of their local economy and the world economy. the bizz was created by world confederation of businesses (worldcob), to offer to the global business community a range of benefits that further their development.worldcob was founded in 2004 in the city of houston, texas, in the united states of america. its primary mission is to promote business development worldwide, recognizing and boosting the growth of leading businesses and businesspeople in every country through the special tools and services that it offers its members. it has approximately 3,000 members representing over 100 countries.sylvan esso at celebrate brooklyn
